## Scripture Reading

Genesis 2:7 (KJV)



<sup class="verse-num">7</sup>And the Lord God formed man of the dust of the ground, and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life; and man became a living soul

## Devotional Reflection

<b><i>We are not just biology; we are spiritual beings. We were made to connect with the One who breathed life into us.

So today, if you feel heavy, remember you are dust. But if you feel worthless, remember you are His breath.</I></b>
