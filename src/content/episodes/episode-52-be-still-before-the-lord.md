## Scripture Reading

<sup class="verse-num">10</sup> Be still, and know that I am God:  
I will be exalted among the heathen,  
I will be exalted in the earth.

---

## Devotional Reflection

As we continue through Great Lent, the pace of life does not automatically slow down.

• **Our schedules remain full.**  
• **Our minds remain noisy.**  
• **Our responsibilities continue.**

And yet Scripture gives us a command that feels almost impossible:

**Be still.**

Today we turn to the Psalms, where stillness is not weakness — but surrender.

Today, intentionally build one moment of stillness.

**No phone.**  
**No music.**  
**No multitasking.**  

Just presence.

Let the quiet feel uncomfortable at first.

**Stay there.**

Stillness is where surrender deepens.

---

## Closing Prayer

*Lord,  
Teach me to be still.  
Calm the restless parts of my heart.  
Quiet my need to control, explain, or defend.  
Help me trust that You are God — even when I am silent.  
Let this Lent reshape my inner noise into peace.  
In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit,  
Amen.*
